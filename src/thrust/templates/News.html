Relativity Space is a private American aerospace manufacturer company headquartered in Los Angeles, California. It was founded in 2015 by Tim Ellis and Jordan Noone. Relativity is developing manufacturing technologies, launch vehicles and rocket engines for commercial orbital launch services.

Things are getting more realistic when in June 2020, Relativity announced that they signed a new launch contract waith Iridium. This contract included up to six dedicated launches to deploy ground spare satellites to LEO(Low Earth Orbit) for Iridium NEXT’s constellation on Relativity’s Terran 1 vehicle. According to Suzi McBride, Iridium's COO, the satellite communication provider chose to partner with Relativity because of their flexible launch capability and the company's ability to launch one satellite at a time. According to the deal these launches will not begin earlier than 2023.
